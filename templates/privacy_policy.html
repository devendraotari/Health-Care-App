Privacy Policy

The data gathered here is only for reference and overall tracking purposes of the spread of the disease across Maharashtra regions, and not for personal identification purposes. Should you be at risk of being a high risk covid patient, the app will direct you with the next steps to contact support. Through the logging of symptoms the app will be able to escalate your risk level once it does reach the threshold for doctors attention needed, and inform you about the same. This app is for us together to combat Covid 19 through responsible self diagnosis in curfew so we can help Stop the spread of the disease.
